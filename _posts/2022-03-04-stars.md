[Watching websites]  
fizzle and rekindle  
just signals in [a net].  
My dog tugs me outside  
for a walk.  
The stars in the sky  
the same as those  
seen by people  
same as me--  
telegraphing above  
[Kharkiv] and [Zaporizhzhia]
